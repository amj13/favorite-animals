My Favorite Animals

This small website introduces three animals that I find interesting and beautiful.

1. Cat

Cats are intelligent and independent animals. Many people keep them as pets because they are clean and playful.

2. Dog

Dogs are known as loyal companions. They help humans in many jobs such as rescue, security, and guiding people.

Reasons why people love these animals

  • They are friendly and loyal
  • They can reduce stress
  • They bring happiness to families

Conclusion

Animals play an important role in human life. Taking care of pets teaches responsibility and kindness.
